Setup Instructions
  1. Open Google Sheets and create a new spreadsheet named Study & Reading Tracker.
  2. Go to Extensions → Apps Script.
  3. Delete the placeholder code and paste the entire contents of the Code.gs file provided alongside this tracker.
  4. Click Save, then Deploy → New Deployment. Type: Web app. Execute as: Me. Who has access: Anyone.
  5. Copy the deployed Web app URL (ends in /exec) and paste it above, then click Save.
  6. Click Push to Sheet for your first sync. The sheet auto-populates with readable tabs: Reading Books, Reading Logs, Study Journal, Scripture Progress, GC Talks, and Scholar Books — plus a _data tab that holds the source-of-truth JSON.
After setup, the tracker auto-syncs 8 seconds after any change in either section. The green dot in the header confirms it's live.
